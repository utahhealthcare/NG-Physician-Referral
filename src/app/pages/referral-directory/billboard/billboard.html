Add this app to your home screen

iOS (iPhone, iPad)

  1. Tap the Share icon.
  2. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. (Optional) Adjust the title.
  4. Tap Add.
  5. A Provider Quick Reference icon appears on your home screen.

Android (Samsung, Motorola, Google, etc.)

  1. Tap the menu (three dots) in the upper‑right.
  2. Select Add to Home screen (or Add shortcut).
  3. A Provider Quick Reference icon appears on your home screen.
